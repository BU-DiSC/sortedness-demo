Sortedness

Real-world data ingestion is rarely perfectly sorted — incoming records arrive nearly sorted, with a small fraction displaced. This demo lets you configure a near-sorted workload and watch two ingestion algorithms — SWARE (buffer-based) and QuIT (tree-based) — process it side by side, comparing their structure and cost in real time.
